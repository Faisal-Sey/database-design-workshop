## Recap (2 min)

Three things to take away:

1. **The Three Questions** — shape of data, access pattern, consistency requirements — pick your DB from these, not from hype.
2. **Schema design** — use real types, foreign keys, and constraints. Normalise to 3NF. Let the DB enforce correctness.
3. **Optimization** — index what you query, read EXPLAIN ANALYZE, know the N+1 problem by name.

## Prompt questions for Q&A

- *"What database does your current project use — would you still pick the same one?"*
- *"Has anyone been bitten by a performance issue they didn't expect?"*
- *"What's one thing you'll change in your next project after today?"*
